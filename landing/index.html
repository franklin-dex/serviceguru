Features

Service Provider Search

ServiceGuru simplifies finding local service providers by allowing users to search based on state, local government area (LGA), and service type. Whether you need plumbing, electrical services, tutoring, or more, ServiceGuru connects you with reliable providers in your area.

Service Provider Registration

ServiceGuru offers an easy-to-use registration feature for service providers looking to expand their reach and connect with potential customers in their area. Whether you're a plumber, tutor, painter, or any other type of service provider, ServiceGuru provides a platform to showcase your services and grow your business.

Search Result

ServiceGuru is dedicated to making the search for reliable service providers as seamless and efficient as possible. Our search result feature is designed to help users quickly find and connect with trusted professionals in their area, ensuring that your needs are met with the highest standards of quality and convenience.

About ServiceGuru

It all started when one of our team members had a frustrating experience trying to find a reliable handyman to fix a faulty laptop. After searching online and asking friends for recommendations, they finally found someone trustworthy. But the process was time-consuming and stressful. We realized that many people face similar struggles when searching for local services.

As a team of tech enthusiasts from Holberton School, we decided to create a platform that simplifies this process for everyone. This project was developed as part of our Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
